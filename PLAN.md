# PLAN

## Product name
AI Explainer App

## Purpose
Turn difficult AI concepts into clear, visual explainers that feel easy to follow and useful to share.

## Target audience
Curious professionals, learners, and mixed-experience teams who want plain-English understanding of AI topics without losing accuracy.

## Current goal
Ship a strong local MVP that reliably generates readable multi-panel AI explainers with consistent characters, solid continuity, and export-ready output.

## What good looks like in the next 2 weeks
- The app feels dependable for common concepts like transformers, fine-tuning, and mixture of experts.
- Generated explainers are clear, visually consistent, and easy to export and save.
- The core generation flow is stable enough to demo without hand-holding.

## Current priorities
- Improve generation reliability and continuity across panels.
- Keep dialogue readable and explanations accurate across modes.
- Tighten the end-to-end local MVP experience: generate, review, save, export.

## Not now
- Multi-user accounts or cloud storage.
- Mobile app or native wrappers.
- Broad deployment/platform work before the local MVP is consistently strong.
